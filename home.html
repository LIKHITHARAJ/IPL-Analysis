INDIAN PREMIER LEAGUE

The INDIAN PREMIER LEAGUE(IPL) is a professional men's Twenty20 cricket league,contested by ten teams based out of ten Indian cities. The league was founded by the BOARD OF CONTROL IN INDIA(BCCI) in 2007. It is usually held between March and May of every year and has exclusive window in the ICC Future Tours Programme.

ROHIT SHARMA

Right-hand Batsman,Right-arm off break Bowler

He was born on 30th April,1987.Rohit is widely popular for his aggressive style of batting where he likes to take the charge to the bowler.
